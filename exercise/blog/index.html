Chelsea Courting Anthony Martial

todays date

With Chelsea facing a fight to keep Eden Hazard and Willian at Stamford Bridge, the Times reports that the Blues have lined up Anthony Martial as a potential replacement. The Manchester United winger outlined his intention to quit Old Trafford at the start of the summer after failing to nail down a regular starting spot under Jose Mourinho. Juventus were thought to be front-runners for the Frenchman, but the Old Lady's record-breaking acquisition of Cristiano Ronaldo has left Martial somewhat in limbo. The 22-year-old might be interested to hear Chelsea's offer, but United's Premier League rivals are apparently unwilling to stump up his €100 million price tag.
